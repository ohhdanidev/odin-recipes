Chewy Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies!

A recipe even the raisin-haters will love! (Well...Hopefully.)

This recipe is for anyone who has bitten into what they thought was a chocolate chip cookie but turned out to be an oatmeal raisin cookie.
This time, there are no raisins, but the oatmeal has decided to stay and chat for a while.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • Half cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 and a quarter cups all-purpose flour
  • Half teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit (165 degrees Celsius)
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into the creamed mixture until just blended
  5. Mix in the quick oats, walnuts, and chocolate chips
  6. Drop by heaping spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets
  7. Bake for 12 minutes in the preheated oven
  8. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely
