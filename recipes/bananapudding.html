Banana Pudding

The perfect dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth!

Are you in a pinch to make a delicious dessert that requires no oven time, and that you can make in less than 30 minutes?

Two words for you: banana pudding. This recipe is all that you need for that sweet tooth. But also, get this: the bigger the bowl, the more banana pudding!

Ingredients

  • 1 5 ounce package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 12 ounce contaner frozen whipped topping
  • 1 16 ounce package vanilla wafers
  • 14 bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat pudding mix and milk for 2 minutes
  2. Blend in condensed milk until smooth
  3. Stir in vanilla extract and fold in whipped topping
  4. Layer wafers, bananas and pudding mixture in a glass serving bowl
  5. Chill until serving
  6. Enjoy!
